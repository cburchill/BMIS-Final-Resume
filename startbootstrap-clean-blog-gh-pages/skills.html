About Me

This is what I am about.

Griffin Colapinto embodies a set of core values centered around mindfulness, meditation, gratitude, journaling, and staying present. Embracing a holistic approach to his life and surfing career, Colapinto prioritizes mental well-being and inner balance alongside physical performance. Through daily meditation practices and mindfulness techniques, he cultivates a sense of presence and awareness, anchoring himself in the present moment and fostering a deeper connection with the ocean and his surroundings.

Gratitude is a cornerstone of Colapinto's philosophy, as he acknowledges the blessings and opportunities in his life with humility and appreciation. Regular journaling serves as a tool for self-reflection and introspection, allowing him to explore his thoughts, emotions, and experiences with honesty and clarity. By embodying these values both on and off the waves, Colapinto not only elevates his performance as a professional surfer but also enriches his life with a sense of purpose, fulfillment, and inner peace.
